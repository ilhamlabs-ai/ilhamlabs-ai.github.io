Emotion-Driven Autonomous Agents

We study how simulated emotions like curiosity and boredom drive AI agents to explore, learn, and create. Our experiments reveal how emotional states shape decision-making and creativity in autonomous systems.

RL & PPO Fine-Tuning for Language Models

We develop and benchmark reinforcement learning approaches including REINFORCE and PPO to fine-tune small language models for creativity, coherence, and diversity.

Open Science & Evaluation

All our research code, metrics, and results are shared openly. We use BLEU, ROUGE, and custom diversity metrics with visualizations and statistical analysis for transparency.
